About ChessForClubs

ChessForClubs is designed to help organize chess tournaments using the Keizer pairing system.

The Keizer system is particularly well suited for club tournaments. It allows players to participate flexibly without requiring them to play every round. Pairings are generated just before each round, making it easy to manage tournaments where attendance may vary.
